Events

Our Events page is your go-to spot for discovering what’s happening at the bookstore. We’re always hosting engaging and exciting events—from author signings and book clubs to storytimes and creative workshops. Our goal is to create meaningful experiences that celebrate stories and ideas. Join us as we bring the local community together through a shared love for stories.

Lavendar Sisters Meeting

Our local Queer Feminist Women’s Book Club is looking for new members! Join us, in the PaperBack Parlor for this week free open meeting, to connect with queer women with a shared liking for reading! Organized by Philippa Quinton, this group meets every two weeks to share insights, stories, and community, empowering and uplifting queer and feminist voices, through thoughtfully chosen thematic reads.
